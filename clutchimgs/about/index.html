Imgs brings you the best pictures on the internet, as provided by the website imgur.com. Join the community by telling the world which ones you like, or even commenting on the ones that move you. We think that these images are the funniest ones on the internet, and we hope you do too!

Imgs Pronunciation Guide: The proper way of pronouncing Imgs is however you want! We think "eye emm gee ess" is fine, as is "images" as is "im giss" and any other thing you can think of. In fact, we invite you to come up with unique ways of saying it.

We built Imgs as a demonstration of the power and flexibility of the Clutch mobile application framework. To learn more about Clutch, visit http://clutch.io/.

This entire application is open sourced! You can browse through the application source, or you can run it yourself over on GitHub. To check it out, simply visit https://github.com/boilerplate/Imgs.
